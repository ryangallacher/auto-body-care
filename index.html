What we do

We specialise in Paintless Dent Removal (PDR), a highly skilled process which removes minor dings and dents from vehicle bodywork, without the costly and time consuming need for traditional bodyshop repairs. We were trained in these techniques in the USA in 1996 and have been fixing dents ever since.

The car trade are our main customers, but we always try our best to serve the general public as well.

Is my dent suitable for PDR?

PDR is suitable for most non-collision dents up to about the size of a tennis ball, providing the paint has not been broken - the type of dent your car picks up in car parks. Accident damage dents, sharp dents, or dents where the paint is damaged are not suitable for PDR.
